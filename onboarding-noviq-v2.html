06 — Onboarding Process

Operations Setup

This phase begins after contract signing. We'll send a spreadsheet with all suggestions and requirements to ensure your property runs safely and profitably from day one.

1

Operational Walkthrough

Audit of all existing and missing items needed for safety and profitability. We document everything and build a plan of action.

2

Procurement & Installation

We'll send a list of required and recommended items — cameras, door locks, closers, Minut detectors, and more. Owners can purchase and install items themselves, or NOVIQ can handle it at cost for labor and materials.

3

Licensing, Insurance & Compliance

We'll guide you through all required short-term rental permits, insurance requirements, and local compliance regulations.

4

Vendor Approval

We'll utilize any vendor contacts you already have first. Then we'll recommend any gaps and/or deploy our in-house maintenance team for any issues.

5

Maintenance Protocol

We fix and replace items under $500 and report all activity in your ownership email thread, with full accounting in invoices.

6

Cleaning Protocol Setup

Our in-house cleaning team is set up to clean your property, manage inventory, and process linens and towels. A mandatory deep clean is required before your first guest. We'll send an estimate for deep and regular cleanings during onboarding.

7

Marketing, Digital Listing & Photography

We arrange professional photography, write SEO-optimized listings, and take video content for social media marketing.

8

Pricing & Positioning

We use market data and comps to set ideal pricing customizations, which we then monitor and adjust daily.

Final Walkthrough & Deep Clean — Launch!

Confirmed with ownership prior to your official launch date.

08 — Cleaning

Our In-House Cleaning Company

NOVIQ owns a cleaning company. Most owners choose to hire us because we match market rates while delivering a higher level of quality and operational control. Everything below is included in the cleaning fee charged to guests.

Supplies & Consumables

  • All cleaning supplies and disposables included: body wash, soap, shampoo, conditioner, paper towels, toilet paper, sponges, coffee, filters, salt & pepper, olive oil, trash bags, and more
  • Supply inventory, storage, stocking, and delivery
  • Dedicated cleaners assigned to your specific property — they know it well

Linens & Towels

  • Linens and towels taken off-site for stain treatment and high-heat washing, then re-delivered
  • NOVIQ-recommended linens are more durable, last longer, and guests don't complain because they are luxurious
  • Bi-yearly linen replacement (approximately every 6 months)

Quality Control

  • Inspections and deep cleanings every 1–2 months (additional charge at owner's expense)
  • Cleaners complete a thorough checklist and upload photos and videos of each cleaning
  • Scheduling and monitoring all cleanings — if a cleaner calls out, we find a replacement immediately

Damage Collections

  • Anytime a stain is found, we immediately attempt to charge the guest — that money goes directly into your account
  • A very involved process — minimum 72 hours with significant labor involved
  • Third-party cleaners: you will be billed separately for all supplies and linens

10 — Your Responsibilities

Owner Responsibilities

NOVIQ handles the day-to-day management — but the following remain the owner's full responsibility. Understanding this clearly helps avoid surprises.

  • Property Preparation for Marketing Interior design, staging, still photography, videography, drone photos, and lifestyle photos. A deep clean prior to NOVIQ onboarding is mandatory, along with the purchase of all NOVIQ-recommended products (linens, towels, amenities, toiletries, disposables, décor, and miscellaneous items).
  • Utilities & Software Subscriptions All utility costs including software (Guesty) and Ring camera subscriptions.
  • Landscaping & Pool / Spa Service Weekly landscaping and twice-weekly pool/spa maintenance service.
  • Repairs, Maintenance & Capital Improvements All costs for repairs, maintenance, capital improvements, and replacement of furnishings, fixtures, and equipment necessary to keep the property in satisfactory condition for rental occupancy.
  • Financial & Tax Obligations All financial matters associated with ownership including property taxes and other property-related taxes.
  • Legal Compliance Ensuring the property is in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. NOVIQ will advise on additional cleaning, repair, or replacement needs — but costs outside our management scope remain the owner's responsibility.

14 — Your Property

Ownership Use Days

You have 7 days per year to book your property for personal use. The only cost to you is the cleaning fee.

To book: send an email in your ownership thread with at least 72 hours advance notice, then book through your Owner Portal. As long as the dates are available, you can book them.

Avoid "Orphan Dates": When booking ownership days, be mindful not to create isolated 1–2 night gaps between your stays and existing bookings. These orphan dates are difficult to fill and will cost you revenue.
