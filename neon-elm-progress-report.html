Section 03
What's Coming Next
These are the next steps to go from tracking visitors to understanding exactly what those visitors are doing — and converting more of them into direct bookings.
Next Step 01

Meta Pixel — Install on Website

A Meta Pixel is a small piece of code added to the website that tells us what visitors do after they arrive. Did they click Book Now? Did they reach the checkout page? This is what unlocks retargeting ads — showing ads specifically to people who already visited the site but didn't book.

Next Step 02

GHL — Past Guest Email Campaign

Using the GHL marketing account, we will send an email to past Neon Elm guests offering a direct booking discount (5–10% off). This is the fastest way to generate direct bookings without ad spend. Guests who already stayed are the most likely to return.

Next Step 03

GHL — Lead Pipeline

Set up a simple pipeline inside GHL to track every inquiry from first contact to confirmed booking. This gives us visibility into how many leads are coming in, where they're dropping off, and what the conversion rate looks like.

Next Step 04

30-Day Report — Traffic Sources

In 30 days, we will have enough data from GA4 and the UTM links to show exactly where website visitors are coming from — Google, Instagram, email, or direct. This will inform where to focus efforts and budget going forward.
